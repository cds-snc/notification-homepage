A platform built for government

Notify is a messaging system built for the Government of Canada.

Send emails or text messages to keep the public informed and make your services more transparent. Use templates to keep communications easy, consistent, and accessible.

Learn more about Notify

Notify is built for you

  • Administrators who have to meet departmental service standards for communication
  • Staff wrestling with multiple systems to provide service updates to the public
  • Developers working on improving workflow and tooling for public servants

What Notify can do

  • Replace Word documents and spreadsheets with a secure database of contacts and messages
  • Save time and meet service standards with templates and shared messaging components
  • Measure the effectiveness of your service with detailed analytics

See how Notify can improve your workflow

Integrate Notify into your system

The Notify API sends messages automatically. Integrate our suite of secure, cloud-based tools with your web application or admin system.

Notify supports roles and permissions, so only staff who need access can send or edit messages.
